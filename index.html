BACKGROUND

The PSHS-SMC DEBATE SOCIETY is an organization in the Philippine Science High School - Southern Mindanao Campus and is one of the ALAs offered by the institution. The organization is composed of students whose passion is to become a debater one day. From Public Speaking to Logical Reasoning, the PSHS-SMC Debate Society got you covered.
